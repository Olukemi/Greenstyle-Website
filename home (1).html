Take up more climate-friendly habits with Greenstyle


A fun activity app to help users integrate more climate friendly habits into their lifestyle by doing various challenges. The goal is to educate the user on their carbon footprint, analyse their individual effects of the lifestyle they choose to live and provide tips, tricks and challenges on how to improve it.

About Us

The Greenstyle app will have various concepts to properly educate and create awareness. The main idea is to have weekly challenges that follow a specific lifestyle in the following categories: Electricity Waste, Resources, Water Reduction, Transportation, and Recycling.

The user is given various tasks each day depending on the category they choose for the week, and is challenged to complete them to the best of their capabilities. Before the weekly challenge, the users are asked for their best estimate of how much they would contribute to the chosen climate change topic, and at the end of the week are given a comparison of this estimate, a report on how they did compared to other users, and advice. By doing so, it demonstrates to the user how much a difference a sustainable lifestyle can make.

This app will also allow users to compete with friends and family to see who can complete the most challenges, or stick to a sustainable lifestyle. This challenge consists of the user trying to preserve water through out the week. A daily challenge could be showering for 5 mins or doing the dishes at the end of the at once.



Water

This challenge consists of the user trying to preserve water through out the week. A daily challenge could be showering for 5 mins or doing the dishes at the end of the week at once.



Electricity

Users will be challenged to save electricity by turn off the lights everytime they leave the room, or challenged to only charge their phone once in a day, which can teach them to preserve battery life.


Food

Food is wasted and so, learning to eat a portion at a time with the option for seconds is a great way to preserve food. The app could suggest a tip like “when eating out ask the waiter for a portion of the food they serve.”


Transportation

Simply carpooling or taking the bus is a great way to transport sustainably. Users will be encouraged to take more sustainable methods of transportation when possible.





Clothing

Many of us buy more clothing than we need. Greenstyle will challenge users to shop at thrift stores or upcycle an old piece of clothing or simply donating unused clothing but not just later buy more clothing.




Resources

Paper, toilet paper, plastic bags/ziplock. Reusing and recycling these resources can reduce carbon footprint, save money and well we can save some trees! Write notes on a document instead of on paper and if you don’t have access to an electronic device, use a whiteboard and take pictures to refer to later.
